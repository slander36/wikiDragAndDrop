wikiDragAndDrop by slander36

First attempt at making a reliable drag and drop wiki markup with HTML5 and CSS3

This is a concept for a drag-and-drop blog/wiki creation that can be easily plugged into any site. It runs in Javascript/HTML5 on the frontend and will use Ajax/JSON for the backend.

Install

Include the files from src and style in your site and direct your page to them. Include a 'content' section in your page and it should start up automatically.
